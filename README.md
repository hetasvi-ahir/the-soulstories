# The SoulStories 📚

The SoulStories is your personal sanctuary for tracking, reviewing, and celebrating your reading journey. Designed with an elegant aesthetic, it helps you organize your library, track series, and visualize your reading statistics.

## ✨ Features

- **Personal Dashboard**: A high-level overview of your reading progress and recent additions.
- **Digital Library**: Manage your entire collection with ease. Search, filter, and organize your books.
- **Visual Bookshelf**: See your books displayed in a beautiful, interactive bookshelf view.
- **Series Tracking**: Keep track of book series and your progress through them.
- **Reading Statistics**: Gain insights into your reading habits with detailed charts and stats.
- **Dark Mode**: A beautiful dark theme for comfortable late-night reading sessions.
- **Cloud Sync**: Powered by Firebase, your library is always synced across your devices.

## 🚀 How to Use the App

Follow these simple steps to get started with The SoulStories:

### 1. Sign In
- Open the app and click on **"Sign in with Google"**.
- This will create your personal account and ensure your library is securely stored in the cloud.

### 2. Add Your First Book
- Click the **"+ Add Book"** button in the sidebar (or the floating plus button on mobile).
- Fill in the book details:
  - **Title & Author**: Essential information.
  - **Status**: Mark it as *To Read*, *Reading*, or *Completed*.
  - **Rating**: Give it a star rating if you've finished it.
  - **Review**: Write down your thoughts.
  - **Cover Image**: Add a URL for the book cover to make your library look great.
  - **Series**: If it belongs to a series, add the series name and volume number.

### 3. Explore Your Library
- Use the **Library** tab to see all your books in a list or grid.
- Use the **Search** bar to find specific titles or authors.
- Click on any book to view its full details, edit its information, or remove it.

### 4. Organize with Series
- Head to the **Series** tab to see your books grouped by their respective series.
- This is perfect for tracking long-running sagas and seeing what's next in your queue.

### 5. Check Your Stats
- Visit the **Statistics** tab to see how many books you've read, your average rating, and other interesting reading patterns.

### 6. Customize Your Experience
- Go to the **Profile** tab to change your display name or toggle between **Light** and **Dark** modes.
